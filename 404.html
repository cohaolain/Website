These Aren't The Droids You're Looking For
The droids you are looking for are currently unavailable. The Imperial network might be experiencing technical difficulties, or you may need to adjust your tracking system.

Please try the following:

  • Click the Rebel Base button, or move along.
  • If you are Luke Skywalker and removed your R2 unit's restraining bolt, make sure that it is still within range.
  • To check your droid caller settings, click the Tattooine menu, and then click Farm Labor Equipment Options. On the Droids tab, click Stolen. The settings should match those provided by your friendly neighborhood Jawas.
  • If the Emperor has enabled it, The Empire can examine your star system and automatically discover droids.
    If you would like to use Stormtroopers to try and discover them, click Detect droids
  • Some droids require 3 Tusken Raiders to "liberate" them. Click the Panic menu and then click About Sand People Diplomacy to determine how easily they will be uninstalled.
  • If you are an Old Hermit Jedi trying to lay low, make sure your options are left wide open as long as possible. Click the Tools menu, and then click on Lightsaber. On the Advanced tab, scroll to the Get Off Tattooine section and check settings for flights to Alderaan.
  • Click the Death Star button if you are Grand Moff Tarkin.
